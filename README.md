# ZInsta
![zinsta](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3ac4c101-8c94-4411-b74b-31afb467852b)

A modern social media app inspired by Instagram, developed with Flutter and Firebase. zInsta aims to provide a seamless and engaging experience for sharing photos and videos, interacting with friends, and discovering new content.

----

## ‚ú® Features

* **User Authentication**: Easily log in/register using your email and password.
* **User CRUD**: Customizable profiles with display images, bios, and posts.
* **Post CRUD**: Easily upload and share photos and videos.
* **Likes, Comments, Save, & Share**: Interact with posts by liking and commenting on them.
* **Follow/Follow Back/Unfollow**: Follow users to see their posts in your feed.
* **Search**: Search for users and discover new content.
* **Push Notifications**: Receive instant notifications when your posts are liked, commented on, or followed.
* **Coversations**: You can send messages betweens your friends, and call him such as video or voice.
